Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about EventFlow AI

How does the AI find prospects on LinkedIn?
Our AI uses advanced search parameters including job titles, industries, company size, location, and past event engagement. It can identify decision-makers based on your ideal customer profile with 95% accuracy. The AI respects LinkedIn's rate limits and uses smart rotation to avoid any issues.
Will the messages sound spammy or robotic?
Absolutely not! Our AI generates personalized messages based on each prospect's profile, recent activity, company news, and pain points. It references specific details (like their latest post or company announcement) to make every message feel genuinely personal. We achieve 3-5x higher response rates than manual outreach.
What types of events work best?
EventFlow AI works for any paid event including: Tech conferences, Startup meetups, Business networking, AI workshops, Corporate training, High-ticket masterminds, Industry summits, and Webinars. The system is particularly effective for B2B events where attendees are professionals on LinkedIn.
How is this different from LinkedIn automation tools?
Unlike basic automation tools that just send generic messages, EventFlow AI is a complete sales system. It researches prospects, personalizes messages, handles conversations, qualifies leads, tracks revenue, and integrates with your CRM. It's like having a full-time SDR, not just a messaging bot.
Can I try it before committing?
Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial on all plans. No credit card required. You'll get full access to all features and can see real results before making any commitment. We also offer a free demo where our team can show you exactly how it works for your specific event type.
What kind of ROI can I expect?
Our customers typically see 3-10x ROI within the first month. Since you're not paying for ads, every ticket sale is pure profit. For example, if your ticket price is ₹2000 and you sell 100 tickets, that's ₹2 lakhs in revenue with zero ad spend. The average customer recovers their investment within the first week.
